
# Topics Overview - Technical Paper within the Domains of Distributed Systems or Industrial Information Technologies

This assignment involves producing a technical paper in the IEEE standard format ([IEEE Templates](https://www.ieee.org/conferences/publishing/templates)), addressing a clearly defined topic within the domains of distributed systems or industrial information technologies. The document must present a structured scientific analysis, describe technical concepts with precision, and explain complex mechanisms using appropriate visualizations.

The paper must consist of **at least four double‑column pages** (excluding the reference section) and follow the conventions of an IEEE publication, including sections such as *Abstract, Introduction, Methodology, Discussion,* and *Results*. All external sources must be properly cited to maintain academic integrity and ensure the traceability of the presented arguments.

A key component of the work is the integration of **explanatory graphics**, such as architectural diagrams, workflow models, or comparative illustrations. These visuals should clarify technical processes and support the conceptual explanations in the text; each graphic must be clearly labeled and referenced.

In addition to the written paper, an **A0 poster presentation** must be created. The *digital poster* should visually summarize the central findings, highlight essential concepts, diagrams, and comparative elements, and be suitable for presentation and exhibition.
